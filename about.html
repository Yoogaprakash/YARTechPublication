Our Story

YAR Tech Publication, a proud initiative under Dharapublishers, was founded with the vision of disseminating technological knowledge that empowers innovation. We curate and publish high-quality content in various fields of technology and engineering, catering to researchers, academicians, and industry professionals.

Our mission at YAR Tech Publication is to ignite intellectual curiosity and facilitate global collaboration by publishing impactful and innovative content across emerging technological domains.

Collaborations
